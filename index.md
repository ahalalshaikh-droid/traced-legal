# Traced — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 9, 2026**

Traced is built on a simple principle: **your thoughts stay on your phone.**

## What we store — and where

Everything you create in Traced — voice recordings, transcribed text, notes,
titles, and folders — is stored **only on your device**. Traced has no
accounts, no cloud database, and no server of ours that your notes are sent
to. We cannot read, access, or recover your notes, because they never reach
us.

Because your data lives only on your device:

- Deleting the app permanently deletes all your notes and recordings.
- Your notes are included in your device's own iCloud/computer backups if
  you have those enabled (this is handled by iOS, not by Traced).
- You can export your data at any time from Settings → Data Export.

## Speech-to-text

Voice notes are transcribed using Apple's speech recognition built into
iOS. Depending on your device and language settings, Apple may process the
audio on-device or on Apple's servers, as described in
[Apple's privacy documentation](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
Traced itself never sends your audio anywhere.

## Purchases

Traced offers an optional one-time purchase (Traced Pro), processed by
Apple. To recognize your purchase and restore it on a new device, we use
[RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy), which receives an
anonymous purchase record (no name, no email, no note content). This is
used only to unlock what you paid for — never for advertising or tracking.

## What we don't do

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising, and no ad identifiers
- No selling or sharing of data — there is nothing to sell
- No accounts, emails, or personal information collected

## Permissions

- **Microphone** — used only while you record a voice note.
- **Speech recognition** — used only to transcribe your voice notes.
- **Notifications** (optional) — used only for the local reminder about
  unsorted thoughts; scheduled on your device, sent by no one.

## Changes

If a future version of Traced changes how data is handled (for example, an
optional AI or sync feature), this policy will be updated first, and any
feature that sends a note off your device will be clearly labeled and
opt-in.
